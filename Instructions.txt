FOLDING INSTRUCTIONS:

● Fold along both long diagonals (solid lines, colored
side out); then unfold.
● Fold the two more-colored sides inward, showing a white
triangle with colored tip, narrow rectangle, and 2 squares.
● Repeat 4 times:
● Fold outer corner up to apex (on dashed line).
● Fold new outermost corner in to center of paper
(along dot-dashed line).
● Fold white tip down to center (along dotted line).
● Tuck small white triangle into nearby pocket.
● Blow into the opening at bottom to inflate. You're done!
